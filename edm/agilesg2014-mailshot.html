12 - 14 November, Marina Bay Sands

Three world-class keynotes, over 35 sessions in Organisation Agility, Agile and Technical Practices
Join us for the second annual Agile Singapore Conference where you'll get to experience three days of learning and interaction with some of the world's most insightful and experienced Product Developers.
