12 - 14 November, Marina Bay Sands

Three world-class keynotes
Over 35 sessions
Join us for the second annual Agile Singapore Conference where you'll get to experience three days of learning, interaction and networking with some of the world's most insightful and experienced Product Developers and your fellow agile enthusiasts from Southeast Asia.

Our keynote speakers this year are Linda Rising, Dave Snowden and Rich Sheridan. In addition we have one of the authors of the Agile Manifesto and over a dozen more speakers who’ve been using and teaching agile approaches for over a decade. They’ll be in Singapore to share their experience and knowledge with more than 400 attendees!
